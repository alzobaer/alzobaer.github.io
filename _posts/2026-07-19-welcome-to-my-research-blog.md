This is the first post on my research blog. I plan to use this space to share
short, informal notes on my work at the intersection of **robotics, computer
vision, and precision agriculture** — the ideas behind the papers, things that
did not make it into them, and lessons learned along the way.

Some topics I hope to write about:

- **Scale-invariant phenotyping** — measuring plant traits in normalized
  render-space, where perspective projection cancels the per-session scale
  ambiguity of Structure-from-Motion.
- **3D Gaussian Splatting** for high-fidelity crop reconstruction, and its
  extension toward 4D (temporal) growth.
- **Autonomous agricultural robotics** — low-cost, self-charging ground
  vehicles for operator-free data acquisition in greenhouses.

Thanks for reading. More soon.
