This is where I write about the ideas behind my research in plain language — plant phenotyping, 3D reconstruction, and the robots that collect the data. Papers are written for reviewers; these posts are written for anyone curious about how you measure a growing plant without ever touching it. Expect short explainers on the core concepts, notes on what did not make it into the papers, and the occasional account of something that went wrong in the greenhouse.
