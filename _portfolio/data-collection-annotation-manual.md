A comprehensive **bilingual (日本語 / English)** manual and toolkit that standardizes
how field data is collected and annotated for 3D plant phenotyping in greenhouse
environments, prepared in the Mineno Laboratory at Shizuoka University.

It covers the full workflow with figures and reference photos:

- **Setup & capture geometry** — camera placement, scale markers, base datum
- **Ground-truth measurement** and reference logging
- **Annotation procedure** and naming/dataflow conventions
- **Quality control** checklist
- **Ready-to-use templates** (CSV + XLSX): annotation, ground-truth, session metadata, sensor log, plant-management log, QC checklist, field log
