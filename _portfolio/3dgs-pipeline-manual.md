A comprehensive, **bilingual (English / 日本語)** manual documenting the full 3D Gaussian
Splatting pipeline for time-series greenhouse crop phenotyping, developed in the Mineno
Laboratory at Shizuoka University.

It walks through the complete workflow:

- **Getting started** — overview, requirements, quick start
- **Environment setup** — CUDA, Python, COLMAP, 3DGS, verification
- **Data preparation** — video requirements, frame extraction, directory structure
- **Pipeline execution** — video processing → COLMAP SfM → 3DGS training → rendering → trait extraction
- **Parameters & troubleshooting** — tuning, common issues, GPU/memory problems
